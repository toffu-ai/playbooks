1. Search LinkedIn for recent posts from each competitor's company page
2. Pull engagement metrics: likes, comments, shares per post
3. Identify top-performing post formats and topics
4. Spot topics your competitors are not covering
5. Log findings to Google Sheets with a content opportunity column
