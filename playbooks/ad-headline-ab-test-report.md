Analyze which ad headlines and descriptions are winning across all my campaigns and which should be retired.

1. Pull all active ads from my Google Ads account with per-ad performance data (impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions, cost)
2. For each ad group that has multiple ads running, compare their performance head-to-head:
   - Which headlines are driving the highest CTR?
   - Which descriptions are driving the most conversions?
   - Which ads have enough data to make a confident call (statistically significant)?
   - Which ads are still too new to judge?
3. Identify clear winners and losers:
   - Ads with significantly lower CTR or conversion rate than the best ad in their group
   - Ads that have been running for 30+ days with no conversions
   - Top-performing ads that should be used as templates for other campaigns
4. Build a Google Sheet with:
   - Ad group, ad headlines, ad descriptions, key metrics
   - Winner/loser/too early designation for each ad
   - Recommended action (keep, pause, iterate)
   - Winning patterns (what do the best ads have in common?)
5. Email me the report with a summary of how much I could save by pausing the losers
6. Schedule a bi-weekly review
