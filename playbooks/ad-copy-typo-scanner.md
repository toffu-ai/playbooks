Scan all my Google Ads headlines and descriptions for typos, grammar issues, and embarrassing mistakes.

1. Pull all active ads from my Google Ads account across every campaign
2. For each ad, review every headline and description for:
   - Spelling mistakes
   - Grammar errors
   - Broken capitalization (random caps, all lowercase where it should not be)
   - Missing or extra punctuation
   - Truncated words or cut-off sentences
   - Repeated words or phrases
3. Create a Google Sheet with:
   - Campaign name, ad group name, ad ID
   - The headline or description with the issue
   - What the issue is
   - Suggested fix
4. Sort by severity (spelling mistakes first, then grammar, then style)
5. Schedule a monthly scan to catch new issues as ads are created
