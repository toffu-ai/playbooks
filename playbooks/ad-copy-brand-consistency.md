Audit all my ad copy to make sure brand names, product names, and key messaging are consistent across every ad.

1. Ask me for my brand guidelines:
   - Correct brand name spelling and capitalization
   - Product names and how they should appear
   - Taglines or phrases that must be used exactly
   - Terms or phrases we should never use
2. Pull all active ads from my Google Ads account
3. Check every headline and description against my guidelines:
   - Brand name misspelled or wrong capitalization
   - Product names inconsistent across ads
   - Outdated taglines or discontinued product references
   - Competitor names mentioned where they should not be
   - Pricing or offers that may be outdated
4. Create a Google Sheet flagging every inconsistency with:
   - The ad location (campaign, ad group, ad ID)
   - What was found vs what it should be
   - Suggested correction
5. Schedule a monthly check
