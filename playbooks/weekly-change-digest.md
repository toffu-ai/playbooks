Comprehensive weekly summary of everything that changed in my Google Ads account, who changed it, and how.

1. Pull all changes from my Google Ads account from the past 7 days
2. Group changes by how they were made:
   - Manual changes (someone in the Google Ads UI or mobile app)
   - API changes (third-party tools or scripts)
   - Auto-applied by Google (recommendation subscriptions)
   - Automated rules
3. Group changes by category:
   - Budget and bid changes (with old and new values)
   - Keyword additions and removals (count and top examples)
   - Ad copy and creative changes
   - Audience and targeting modifications
   - Campaign structure changes (new campaigns, paused campaigns)
4. Highlight the top 5 most impactful changes based on spend affected
5. Flag anything unusual:
   - Changes made outside business hours
   - Changes by unrecognized users
   - Unusually high volume of changes on a single campaign
6. Build the digest in a Google Sheet and email it to me with subject "Google Ads Weekly Change Digest - [DATE]"
7. Schedule weekly execution every Monday morning
