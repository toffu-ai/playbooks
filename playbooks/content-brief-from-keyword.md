1. Take a target keyword as input
2. Pull the top 10 ranking pages and extract their H2 structure and key topics
3. Identify questions from People Also Ask and related searches
4. Draft a brief: target keyword, secondary keywords, word count target, required sections, angle/differentiation
5. Export the brief to Google Docs or Sheets, ready for a writer
