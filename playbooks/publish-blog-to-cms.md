1. Receive a topic or keyword target as input
2. Research top-ranking pages and pull key points to cover
3. Write a full blog post: H1, intro, 4-6 sections with H2s, conclusion, meta description
4. Optimize for the target keyword: title tag, headers, internal links
5. Publish directly to your CMS (Shopify, WordPress, Ghost, or Webflow)
