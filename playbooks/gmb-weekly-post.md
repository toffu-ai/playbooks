1. Pull recent product updates, promotions, or blog posts to use as source material
2. Write a Google Business post: 150-300 words, conversational tone, clear CTA
3. Select or generate an appropriate image
4. Publish directly to your Google Business Profile
5. Log the post and schedule a reminder to check engagement next week
