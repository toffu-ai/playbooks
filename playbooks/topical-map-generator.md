1. Fetch your sitemap and extract all published URLs with their titles
2. Group pages into topic clusters using their content and URL structure
3. Identify clusters with thin coverage (1-2 pages) vs. strong coverage (5+)
4. Pull GSC impressions per cluster to find high-potential areas with low coverage
5. Output a topical map to Google Sheets: clusters, page count, impression volume, and gap score
