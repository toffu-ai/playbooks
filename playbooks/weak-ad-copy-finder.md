Find ads with weak copy that is hurting my click-through rate and suggest stronger alternatives.

1. Pull all active ads from my Google Ads account with their performance metrics (impressions, clicks, CTR, conversions)
2. Identify ads with below-average CTR compared to other ads in the same ad group
3. Analyze the underperforming ad copy for common weaknesses:
   - Generic headlines with no specific value proposition ("Best Service", "Click Here")
   - Missing call-to-action
   - No urgency or differentiator
   - Headlines that do not match the keywords in the ad group
   - Descriptions that repeat the headline instead of adding value
4. For each weak ad, write 3 alternative headline and description variations that:
   - Include a clear benefit or differentiator
   - Match the search intent of the ad group keywords
   - Use proven copywriting patterns (numbers, questions, urgency)
5. Create a Google Sheet with the weak ads, their metrics, the issues found, and the suggested alternatives
6. Schedule a monthly review
