# Lead Nurturing Email Campaign

### 5-Email Sequence
1. Welcome Email
   - Introduce your brand
   - Set expectations

2. Value Proposition
   - Highlight key benefits
   - Share success stories

3. Social Proof
   - Customer testimonials
   - Case studies

4. Special Offer
   - Limited time promotion
   - Clear call to action

5. Final Call to Action
   - Urgency elements
   - Next steps
