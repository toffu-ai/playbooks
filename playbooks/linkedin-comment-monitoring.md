1. Fetch your recent LinkedIn posts and their comment counts
2. Pull all new comments since the last check
3. Classify comments: question, positive feedback, objection, or spam
4. Draft a thoughtful reply for each question and objection
5. Post replies directly or present drafts for review before publishing
